7x5D Matrix

      

        

          
            

              Dimension
              Main Question
              Distortion Risk
              Developmental Possibility
            

          
          
            

              D1 Domains
              Which philosophical, historical, legal, economic, cultural, technological, ecological, and relational domains are active?
              Reducing the issue to one cause or one discipline.
              Multi-domain public reasoning.
            

            

              D2 Thinking Modes
              Which conceptual, contextual, comparative, critical, and creative modes are strong or missing?
              Logical clarity without context, or critique without alternatives.
              Balanced and imaginative judgment.
            

            

              D3 Formation Arc
              What must be discerned, disrupted, disclosed, displaced, and practiced?
              Insight that never becomes accountable civic participation.
              Disciplined participation.
            

            

              D4 5C Capacities
              Does the discourse cultivate critical, creative, communicative, collaborative, and compassionate capacity?
              Critique without compassion, or collaboration without justice.
              Responsible civic formation.
            

            

              D5 Learning Arc
              Does knowledge become study, understanding, wisdom, and transformative action?
              Information mistaken for wisdom.
              Embodied learning.
            

            

              D6 Becoming
              What kind of political subject is being formed?
              Passive receiver, loyal partisan, or resentful opponent.
              Architect-steward of shared life.
            

            

              D7 Embodied Integration
              What happens to bodies, families, neighborhoods, institutions, ecosystems, and future generations?
              Abstract principle detached from human consequence.
              Life-giving shared world.
